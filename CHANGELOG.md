##  **Tuff Soul Labs | Official Changelog**

### **[2.1] - 2026-04-22 (Stable Release)**
*The "Stability & Efficiency" Update.*

**System Optimization & Resource Management**
* **Idle-State Hibernate:** Implemented a new intelligent session-management system. The application now automatically terminates inactive background processes to conserve system memory (RAM) and optimize power consumption when the interface is closed or unresponsive.
* **Engine Refinement:** Enhanced the core download engine for consistent high-speed data transfer and lower CPU overhead.

**Global Communication & Security**
* **Broadcast System:** Integrated a new encrypted notification framework to provide real-time updates and global service announcements directly within the ecosystem.
* **Local-First Privacy:** Reaffirmed the "Zero-Knowledge" architecture. All downloaded content and session data are strictly localized to the user’s machine. 

**Bug Fixes**
* Resolved an issue where legacy processes remained active after session termination.

---

### **[2.0] - 2026-04-17 (Legacy Build)**
*The "Interface Revolution" Update.*

**Visual & User Experience (UX)**
* **Signature "Aura" UI:** Introduced a premium, high-fidelity interface featuring the Tuff Soul deep-purple aesthetic.
* **Tri-Tier Queue Management:** Deployed a comprehensive task-management system with dedicated streams for **Active**, **Completed**, and **Failed** tasks.
* **Live Metrics:** Added real-time telemetry, including Download Speed, ETA, and Storage Estimation.

**Expanded Media Support**
* **Universal Compatibility:** Extended support for major media platforms (including Instagram and YouTube).
* **High-Fidelity Output:** Added support for 4K video resolution and lossless audio formats (WAV, MP3).
* **High-Traffic Optimization:** Optimized the scraping engine to maintain maximum quality during peak traffic periods on source platforms.

**Support Integration**
* **Helpdesk :** Integrated a secure feedback portal for direct bug reporting and feature requests .

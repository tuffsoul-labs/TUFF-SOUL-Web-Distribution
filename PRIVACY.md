# Privacy & Security Protocol
## Tuff Soul Labs | Vision 2.1

At **Tuff Soul Labs**, we believe that high-performance utility should never come at the cost of personal privacy. Our architecture is built on a **Local-First** philosophy: your creative output and activity remain on your machine, where they belong.

To maintain the integrity of our ecosystem and ensure peak performance for all users, we collect limited, non-identifiable technical telemetry. Below is a transparent breakdown of how we protect you and the platform.

---

### 1. Technical Telemetry (Security & Stability)
We monitor essential system metrics to prevent automated bot attacks and ensure the software remains stable across different hardware configurations.

* **Device Authorization (HWID & Device Name):** We use a unique hardware identifier to authorize the application session. This prevents bot interference and unauthorized distribution, ensuring that the resources are available for real users.
* **Performance Metrics (Session Runtime):** We track how long the application is active to identify memory leaks or crashes, allowing us to optimize power consumption and system efficiency.
* **Operational Success Rates:** We monitor general success and failure counts of tasks to identify if a platform update (e.g., a change in a site's layout) has impacted engine features.

### 2. Regional Context (Service Optimization)
To ensure our servers and scrapers are performing at maximum speed for your area, we log broad geographic data.

* **Location Scope:** We only see the **State and Country** from which the app is operated. 
* **Zero Precision:** We do **not** collect GPS data, IP addresses, or street-level information. This data is used solely to balance traffic and comply with regional digital standards.

### 3. The "Zero-Knowledge" Guarantee
Our commitment to your anonymity is hard-coded into our software.

* **No Activity Logging:** We do **not** monitor, store, or track *what* you download. We have no visibility into your URLs, file names, or media content.
* **No Personal Identifiers:** We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any data that could link a session to a specific human identity.
* **Local Storage:** All media files and user-generated content are stored exclusively on your local hardware. Tuff Soul Labs has no remote access to your files.

### 4. Data Usage & Security
The limited telemetry we collect is used exclusively for:
1.  Patching bugs and technical errors.
2.  Improving download speeds for specific regions.
3.  Protecting the community from malicious bot activity.

---

### Community Trust
Tuff Soul Labs is dedicated to providing "Performance without Compromise." By being transparent about our minimal technical requirements, we aim to build a platform that users can trust for the long term.
